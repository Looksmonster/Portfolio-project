About me

User Interface and User Experience and Also video editing

I'm Nikita Soboliev, a passionate UI/UX Design student at Orbis School. From an early age, I’ve been fascinated by how design can shape the way people interact with technology. Currently, I’m improving my skills in Figma and Adobe Photoshop, creating clean, modern, and user-centered interfaces. At the same time, I ’m developing my technical side — working with HTML, CSS, and Python. This combination of design and development allows me to not only create beautiful visuals, but also understand how things work under the hood. Whether it’s designing a mobile app, building a responsive website, or editing videos to bring ideas to life — I love the entire creative process. I pay great attention to details, typography, and user experience, always striving to make interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and enjoyable to use. Always open to new challenges, learning opportunities, and interesting projects.
